    Conditional Form Conjugation Rules

    Conditional Form Conjugation Rules

    In this lesson, we will learn how to change from dictionary form to conditional form to express condition in Japanese.

    Rule 1: For u-verbs: Replace the u-vowel sound with the え equivalent and attach ば

    Rule 2: For ru-verbs: Replace る with れば

    Rule 3: For verb exceptions: する becomes すれば and くる becomes くらば

    Rule 4: For い-Adj: Change the last い of the い-adjective into ければ

    Rule 5: For な-Adj: Change the last な of the な-adjective and attach なら

    Rule 6: For Noun: Attach なら to the noun

    Rule 1


    For u-verbs: Replace the u-vowel sound with the え equivalent and attach ば

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Conditional Form
    使（つか）う Replace う with the え equivalent and attach ば 使（つか）えば
    引（ひ）く Replace く with the え equivalent and attach ば 引（ひ）けば
    泳（およ）ぐ Replace ぐ with the え equivalent and attach ば 泳（およ）げば
    起（お）こす Replace す with the え equivalent and attach ば 起（おこ）せば
    待（ま）つ Replace つ with the え equivalent and attach ば 待（ま）てば
    往（おう）ぬ Replace ぬ with the え equivalent and attach ば 往（おう）ねば
    呼（よ）ぶ Replace ぶ with the え equivalent and attach ば 呼（よ）べば
    読（よ）む Replace む with the え equivalent and attach ば 読（よ）めば
    走（はし）る Replace る with the え equivalent and attach ば 走（はし）れば

    Rule 2

    For ru-verbs: Replace る with れば

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Conditional Form
    食（た）べる replace る with れば 食（た）べれば

    Rule 3

    For exceptions: する becomes すれば and くる becomes くれば

    Dictionary Form Conditional Form
    する すれば
    くる くれば

    Rule 4

    For い-Adj: Change the last い of the い-adjective into ければ

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Conditional Form
    寒（さむ）い replace the last い of the い-adjective into ければ 寒（さむ）ければ

    Rule 5

    For な-Adj: Change the last な of the な-adjective and attach なら

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Conditional Form
    元気（げんき）[な] replace the last な of the な-adjective and attach なら 元気（げんき）なら

    Info

    You should attach であれば to な-Adj when you want to use it for writing report/formal write up because なら form is usually used in conversion.

    Rule 6

    For Noun: Attach なら to the noun

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Conditional Form
    雨（あめ） attach なら 雨（あめ）なら

    Info

    You should attach であれば to Noun when you want to use it for writing report/formal write up because なら form is usually used in conversion.
