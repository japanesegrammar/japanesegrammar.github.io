    Conditional - ば (Ba)

    When using conditional form, first part of the sentence describes the requirement for something to happen; just like IF in English.

    Prerequisite

    Please see these rules if you want to know how to do verb conjugation for conditional form.

    Example A : U-Verb
    In this example, we should convert 押す to 押せ to get え equivalent sound. And then attach ば to obtain the final form 押せば.
  1. ボタンを 押（お）せば、 窓（まど）が 開（あ）きます。
    2. If you press the button, the window will open.

    Example B: U-Verb
    In this example, we should convert 行く to 行け to get え equivalent sound. And then attach ば to obtain the final form 行けば.
  2. 彼（かれ）が 行（い）けば、私（わたし）も行（い）きます。
    3. If he goes, I will go too.

    Example C: Ru-Verb
    In this example, we convert 食べる to 食べ and attach れば to get the final form 食べれば.
  3. これを食（た）べれば 健康（けんこう）になる。
    4. If you eat this, you will be healthy.

    Example D: Suru-Verb
    In this example, we should convert する dictionary form to the final form すれば.
  4. 電話（でんわ）すれば よかったのに。
    5. You should've called.

    Example E: I-Adjective
    In this example, we change from 安い to 安 and attach ければ to get the final form 安いければ.
  5. もう 少（すこ）し 安（やす）ければ．．．
    6. If a little cheaper. . .

    Example F: Na-Adjective
    In this example, we are attaching なら to 暇 (Na-Adjective) to obtain the final form 暇なら.
  6. 暇（ひま）なら、手伝（てつだ）ってください。
    7. If you are free, please help.

    Example G: Noun
    In this example, we are attaching なら to 天気 (Noun) to get the final form 天気なら.
  7. いい 天気（てんき）なら、向（む）こうに 島（しま）が 見（み）えます。
    8. When it is fine, an island can be seen over there.

    Vocabulary

    1. 私（わたし） ⇌ I; me​
    2. 彼（かれ） ⇌ he; him
    3. 食（た）べる ⇌ to eat
    4. 手伝（てつだ）う ⇌ to help; to assist
    5. 行（い）く ⇌ to go; to move
    6. 安（やす）い ⇌ cheap; calm; peaceful; quiet
    7. 押（お）す ⇌ to push; to press​
    8. 窓（まど） ⇌ window​
    9. 開（ひら）く ⇌ to open
    10. 健康（けんこう） ⇌ health​; health​y
    11. 電話（でんわ） ⇌ telephone call; phone call​; telephone
    12. 暇（ひま） ⇌ spare time; free time
    13. 天気（てんき） ⇌ weather
    14. 向（む）こう ⇌ over there; that way; far away
    15. 島（しま） ⇌ island​
    16. 見（み）える ⇌ to be seen; to be in sight​
