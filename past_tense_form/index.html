    Past Tense Verb Conjugation Rules

    Past Tense Verb Conjugation Rules

    Rule 1: For ru-verbs: Replace る with た
    Rule 2:
      For u-verbs ending with う, つ and る: Replace those ending with った
      For u-verbs ending with む, ぶ and ぬ: Replace those ending with んだ
      For u-verbs ending with く: Replace those ending with いて
      For u-verbs ending with ぐ: Replace those ending with いだ
      For u-verbs ending with す: Replace those ending with した
      u-verbs exception: past tense form of 行く is 行った
    Rule 3: For exceptions: する becomes した and くる becomes きた

    Rule 1

    For ru-verbs: Replace る with た

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Past Tense Form
    食（た）べる Replace る with た 食（た）べた

    Rule 2

    For u-verbs: we have 5 subs categories.

    u-verbs with final う, つ and る

    For u-verbs ending with う, つ and る, we should replace with った.

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Past Tense Form
    会（あ）う Replace う with った 会（あ）った
    待（ま）つ Replace つ with った 待（ま）った
    とる Replace る with った とった

    u-verbs with final む, ぶ and ぬ

    For u-verbs ending with む, ぶ and ぬ, we should replace with んだ.

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Past Tense Form
    読（よ）む Replace む with んで 読（よ）んだ
    遊（あそ）ぶ Replace ぶ with んで 遊（あそ）んだ
    死（し）ぬ Replace ぬ with んで 死（し）んだ

    u-verbs with final く

    For u-verbs ending with く, we should replace with いて.

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Past Tense Form
    書（か）く Replace く with いた 書（か）いた

    Info

    There is exception in this class. The て-form of 行（い）くis 行（い）った.

    u-verbs with final ぐ

    For u-verbs ending with ぐ, we should replace with いで.

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method て-Form
    泳（およ）ぐ Replace ぐ with いだ 泳（およ）いだ

    u-verbs with final す

    For u-verbs ending with す, we should replace with した.

    Dictionary Form Replacing Method Past Tense Form
    話（はな）す Replace す with した 話（はな）した

    Rule 3

    For irregular verbs する and くる, we should replace as the following

    Dictionary Form て-Form
    する した
    くる きた
